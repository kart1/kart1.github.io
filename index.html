Capital Karts Lap Time Leaderboard

We use a state of the art time tracking system to ensure your lap times are recorded accurately. We all have a competitive streak in us and like to beat others on the track. We feel that we should reward the fastest lap times of the past seven days by displaying them on our leaderboard so you can go and brag to all your friends with evidence to back you up.
