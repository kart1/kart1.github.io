Kart1 Go Karting HCM

Conveniently located just 10 minutes from the Central and with easy access via motobike, car & public transport, it’s no wonder we are the first choice for Go Karting HCM.

If you’re looking for an exhilarating go karting experience in the heart of HCM look no further than Kart1. Fully equipped with a 350m track and nine challenging corners, you’ll be able to show your racing skill in our impressive track.
